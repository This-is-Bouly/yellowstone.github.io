Menoflow is operated by Yellowstone Creative LLC ("Company", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Menoflow mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect certain information to provide, personalize, and improve the Menoflow service.

1.1 Information You Provide

When using the App, you may voluntarily provide information such as:

  • Age or age range
  • Lifestyle information (sleep, diet, movement, stress levels)
  • Health and wellness information (symptoms, menopause-related symptoms)
  • Responses to questionnaires
  • Preferences and settings within the app

This information allows Menoflow to provide personalized wellness insights and recommendations. Menoflow does not require users to provide their real name.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage data
  • Crash reports
  • Interaction with app features

This information helps us maintain and improve the functionality and reliability of the App.

2. Health Information

Some information entered in the App may relate to health or wellness, such as symptoms or lifestyle habits. This information is used solely to generate personalized wellness insights and educational content within the App.

Menoflow does not provide medical diagnoses or medical treatment, and the App should not be considered a substitute for professional medical advice.

3. How We Use Your Information

Yellowstone Creative LLC may use collected information to:

  • Provide personalized insights and content within the App
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Monitor app performance and stability
  • Analyze usage trends to improve the service
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse of the App

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Menoflow may use trusted third-party services to help operate and improve the App, including services that provide analytics, performance monitoring, and crash reporting. These services may collect anonymized technical information about how the App is used. Each third-party provider processes data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Yellowstone Creative LLC takes reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the services of the App and to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Users may request deletion of their information at any time.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, including under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other privacy laws, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Children's Privacy

Menoflow is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that information from a child has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your country of residence. When this occurs, we implement appropriate safeguards in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
